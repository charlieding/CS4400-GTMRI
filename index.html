Frequently Asked Questions



Who are we?
We are 4 undergraduate college students studying computer science at Georgia Institute of Technology who are creating a new GTMRI system.

What problem you want to solve?
We want an easy to use application for doctors and patients.

Where the problem is happening?
This problem is happening nationwide. Everyone needs this.

Why the audience should care?
Safer, Faster, Better. Done Deal.

How you propose solving the problem?
We are making a web application that will suit all of the doctors and patient's needs.
